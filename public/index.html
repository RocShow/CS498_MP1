Who Am I?
I am a geek who believes computer and the Internet making our world better. I got a bachelor degree of Computer Science and Technology at China Agricultural University (CAU). Now I am pursuing a master degree of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I will graduate in December, 2015. I interned at Sogou.com as a Software Development Engineer (SDE) in 2013. I did research in the Lab of Computer Vision at CAU from 2012 to 2014. Also, I made several apps, add-ons, and plug-ins to make my life easier. All this experience convinced me to believe that computer and Internet can make our life better. With this belief as well as solid professional skills I've learnt from courses and practical development experience, I am ready to do the most challenging and most amazing products.
